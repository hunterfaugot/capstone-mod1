Game Guide

  • Click Assemble to manufacture machine chips.
  • Soon you'll have enough to build Processing Units (PUs) and Assembly Modules (AMs).
  • Combine these to create robots to auto assemble chips.
  • Craft miner robots to excavate rare materials to build new parts and even better robots!
  • Tip: Double-tapping spacebar can be quicker than clicking...
